# Code Of Conduct

TradingSpy should be a practical, curious, and respectful place to work on trading research tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Keep disagreement technical and evidence-based.
- Help make failures reproducible.
- Remember that backtests and generated strategies are research artifacts, not financial advice.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private data, credentials, or paid/proprietary content without permission.
- Presenting unverified trading results as guaranteed outcomes.
- Encouraging users to bypass safety checks or expose remote agent outputs without trust/auth.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that make the project unsafe or hostile. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocking from the project.
