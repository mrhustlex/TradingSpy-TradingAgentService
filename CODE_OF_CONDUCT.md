# Code of Conduct

TradingSpy should be a practical, curious, and respectful place to build trading-research tools.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Keep disagreement technical and evidence-based.
- Help make problems reproducible.
- Respect privacy, credentials, licenses, and proprietary data.
- Present backtests and generated strategies as research artifacts, never guaranteed outcomes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disclosure of private information, and sharing credentials or paid/proprietary content without permission are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to keep the project safe and welcoming. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer.
